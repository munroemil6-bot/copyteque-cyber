Meet the Owner

Moses Wanjala Laisa

Moses Wanjala Laisa is the founder and proprietor of Copyteque — a professional, customer-focused cyber and office supplies business serving individuals, schools, and organizations across Bungoma County.

With a commitment to quality, affordability, and swift service, Copyteque has become a reliable destination for both walk-in cyber users and institutions requiring bulk office supplies. Whether you need a quick printout or a complete supply of toner cartridges for your school, Copyteque is your go-to stop.
